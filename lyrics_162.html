Nanda Ke Ananda Bhaiyo Song Name: Nanda Ke AnandaBhaiyo (1) nanda ke ānanda bhāiyo jayakanhaiyā lāl kī jaya kanhaiyā lāl kī jaya kanhaiyālāl kī (2) hathi dīnī ghoḍā dīnī ane dīnīpālakī nanda ke ānanda bhāiyo jayakanhaiyā lāl kī (3) javāneń ko hāthi ghoḍe buḍḍheńko palki nanda ke ānanda bhāiyo jayakanhaiyā lāl kī TRANSLATION 1) All glories to Kanhaiya Lal who is the bliss of NandaMaharaj, my brothers. 2) Nanda in happiness gave elephants and horses andpalanquins. 3) To the young people he gave horses and elephants, to theold people palanquins.
